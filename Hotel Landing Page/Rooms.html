Hollywood Twin Room


Hollywood Twin rooms feature two single beds lined up next to each other, allowing the room to be used as a twin or double. Desks in the rooms also make them ideal for single business travelers.


Deluxe Room

Deluxe rooms are usually larger than their standard counterparts, may include a bathtub and a shower in the bathroom, and include more high-end amenities.
